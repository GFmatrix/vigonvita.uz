Vigonvita Life Sciences Co., Ltd

Vigonvita Life Sciences Co., Ltd, established in January 2013, is a biomedicine company, focusing on the field of neuropsychiatric system and infectious disease treatment. The company has undertaken a number of national, provincial and ministerial science and technology projects and won honors such as “National High-tech Enterprise”, “ Leading enterprise in Suzhou Industrial Park”, “ Small and medium-sized scientific and technological enterprise in Jiangsu Province”.

Vigonvita has a complete innovative medicine research and development system. We’ve set up R&D centers in Suzhou, Shanghai and Nantong, and set up a technical team with rich experience in medicine R&D. The R&D personnel account for 75% of the company's total personnel.

To improve industrialization, Vigonvita Life Sciences Co., Ltd and Suzhou Institute of Material Medica jointly funded the establishment of Vigonvita (Lianyungang) Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd, which serves as a production site for the company's innovative medicines with its annual production capacity of 450 million capsules (or 1.6 billion tablets). Also, the supporting API production workshop will soon be completed and put into use. The multi-functional modular production platform is under construction for emergencies.
